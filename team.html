Meet Our Team

Dedicated professionals committed to your mental health and wellness

Our Compassionate Experts

At ReThink Mental Health, our team consists of highly trained professionals with diverse backgrounds and specialties. We work collaboratively to provide comprehensive, personalized care that addresses your unique needs.

Each member of our team is committed to staying at the forefront of mental health care, continuously updating their knowledge and skills to offer you the most effective treatments available.

Leadership Team

Dr. Sarah Johnson Photo

Dr. Sarah Johnson, MD

Founder & Medical Director

Board Certified in Psychiatry, Fellowship in Integrative Medicine

Dr. Johnson founded ReThink Mental Health with a vision to transform mental health care through an integrative approach. With over 15 years of experience in psychiatry, she specializes in treatment-resistant mood disorders and PTSD, with particular expertise in ketamine therapy and other innovative treatments.

Prior to founding ReThink, Dr. Johnson served as the Chief of Psychiatry at Denver Memorial Hospital and has published numerous research papers on integrative approaches to mental health.

Specialties:

Ketamine Therapy Treatment-Resistant Depression PTSD Integrative Psychiatry
Dr. Michael Chen Photo

Dr. Michael Chen, MD

Clinical Director

Board Certified in Psychiatry, Addiction Medicine

Dr. Chen oversees the clinical operations at ReThink Mental Health, ensuring that all patients receive the highest quality care. He specializes in addiction psychiatry and co-occurring disorders, bringing a wealth of experience in treating complex cases.

With a background in both academic and clinical settings, Dr. Chen is passionate about implementing evidence-based practices while remaining open to innovative approaches that can benefit patients who haven't responded to traditional treatments.

Specialties:

Addiction Medicine Co-occurring Disorders Medication Management Spravato Treatment
Lisa Rodriguez Photo

Lisa Rodriguez, MSN, PMHNP-BC

Director of Wellness Services

Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, Certified in Functional Medicine

Lisa leads our wellness initiatives, bringing together her expertise in psychiatric care and functional medicine. She developed our comprehensive wellness program, including nutritional counseling and micronutrient therapy protocols.

With a holistic approach to mental health, Lisa is dedicated to addressing the underlying factors that contribute to mental health conditions, helping patients achieve sustainable wellness through lifestyle modifications and targeted interventions.

Specialties:

Functional Medicine Nutritional Psychiatry Hormone Balance Wellness Injections

Psychiatrists & Psychiatric Providers

Dr. James Wilson Photo

Dr. James Wilson, MD

Psychiatrist

Board Certified in Psychiatry, Specialization in First Responder Mental Health

Dr. Wilson specializes in treating first responders, military personnel, and frontline healthcare workers. With a background as a military psychiatrist, he brings unique insight into the mental health challenges faced by these communities.

His approach combines evidence-based treatments with a deep understanding of the cultural contexts and specific stressors that affect these populations, creating a safe and effective treatment environment.

Specialties:

First Responder Care PTSD Trauma-Informed Care Resilience Building
Dr. Aisha Patel Photo

Dr. Aisha Patel, MD

Psychiatrist

Board Certified in Psychiatry and Child & Adolescent Psychiatry

Dr. Patel specializes in child, adolescent, and young adult psychiatry, bringing a developmental perspective to mental health care. She is skilled in diagnosing and treating conditions that often first appear in childhood and adolescence.

With a warm, engaging approach, Dr. Patel creates a comfortable environment for young patients and their families, helping them navigate the challenges of mental health conditions during crucial developmental periods.

Specialties:

Child & Adolescent Psychiatry ADHD Anxiety Disorders Family Therapy
Robert Thompson Photo

Robert Thompson, PA-C

Psychiatric Physician Assistant

Certified Physician Assistant, Specialized Training in Psychiatric Care

Robert provides comprehensive psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and follow-up care for adults with a wide range of mental health conditions. He takes a collaborative approach to treatment, working closely with patients to develop personalized care plans.

With a background in primary care before specializing in psychiatry, Robert brings a holistic perspective to mental health, considering both physical and psychological factors in his assessment and treatment recommendations.

Specialties:

Medication Management Depression Anxiety Disorders Bipolar Disorder

Specialized Care Providers

Dr. Thomas Reynolds Photo

Dr. Thomas Reynolds, MD

Ketamine Specialist

Board Certified in Anesthesiology, Specialized Training in Ketamine Therapy

Dr. Reynolds oversees our ketamine therapy program, bringing extensive experience in anesthesiology and ketamine administration. He ensures that all ketamine treatments are delivered safely and effectively, with protocols tailored to each patient's needs.

Having worked with ketamine in both surgical and psychiatric settings, Dr. Reynolds has a unique understanding of its mechanisms and therapeutic potential for treatment-resistant conditions.

Specialties:

Ketamine Infusion Therapy Treatment-Resistant Depression PTSD Chronic Pain
Emma Lawson Photo

Emma Lawson, MS, RD, LDN

Nutritional Psychiatry Specialist

Registered Dietitian, Certified in Nutritional Psychology

Emma specializes in the relationship between nutrition and mental health, helping patients understand how dietary choices can impact mood, cognition, and overall mental wellness. She develops personalized nutrition plans that complement psychiatric treatment.

With advanced training in nutritional approaches to mental health conditions, Emma stays current with the latest research on the gut-brain connection and nutritional interventions for psychiatric disorders.

Specialties:

Nutritional Psychiatry Gut-Brain Connection Anti-inflammatory Diets Food Sensitivities
Dr. Marcus Lee Photo

Dr. Marcus Lee, PhD

Neuropsychologist

Licensed Clinical Psychologist, Board Certified in Neuropsychology

Dr. Lee conducts comprehensive neuropsychological assessments to evaluate cognitive functioning, helping to diagnose conditions like ADHD, learning disorders, and cognitive effects of mental health conditions or treatments.

His detailed assessments provide valuable insights that inform treatment planning and help patients better understand their cognitive strengths and challenges, leading to more targeted and effective interventions.

Specialties:

Neuropsychological Testing ADHD Assessment Learning Disorders Cognitive Rehabilitation
