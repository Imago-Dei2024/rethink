Integrative Approach to Mental Wellness

At ReThink Mental Health, we believe that mental wellness is deeply connected to physical health. Our integrative approach addresses both mind and body, offering services that support overall wellness alongside traditional psychiatric care.

Our wellness services are designed to complement your mental health treatment, addressing nutritional deficiencies, supporting cellular health, and promoting lifestyle changes that enhance your overall wellbeing.

Wellness Injections

Micronutrient & Vitamin Injections

Nutritional deficiencies can significantly impact mental health. Our targeted micronutrient injections deliver essential vitamins, minerals, and amino acids directly into the bloodstream, bypassing digestive issues that can limit absorption of oral supplements.

These injections can help address deficiencies that may contribute to depression, anxiety, fatigue, and cognitive issues. They're also beneficial for overall wellness and immune support.

NAD+ Therapy

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme found in all living cells that plays a crucial role in cellular energy production and DNA repair. NAD+ levels naturally decline with age and stress, which can impact cognitive function, energy levels, and overall health.

Our NAD+ therapy can help:

  • Improve cognitive function and mental clarity
  • Support addiction recovery
  • Increase energy levels
  • Reduce inflammation
  • Support cellular repair and anti-aging processes

The Mind-Gut Connection

Research increasingly shows that gut health is directly linked to brain health and mental wellness. Our nutrition counseling services help you understand this connection and make dietary changes that support both physical and mental health.

Our Nutrition Services

Our registered dietitian specializes in nutritional approaches for mental health and works closely with our psychiatric team to develop personalized nutrition plans that complement your mental health treatment.

We Can Help With:

  • Anti-inflammatory diets for depression and anxiety
  • Nutritional strategies for ADHD
  • Gut health optimization
  • Food sensitivities and their impact on mood
  • Blood sugar management for mental stability
  • Nutrient-dense meal planning
  • Supplement recommendations

What to Expect

Your nutrition journey begins with a comprehensive assessment of your current diet, health history, and mental health concerns. From there, we'll develop a personalized nutrition plan that fits your lifestyle, preferences, and therapeutic goals. Follow-up sessions provide support, accountability, and adjustments as needed.

A Note About Supplements

While supplements can be valuable tools in supporting mental health, they should be used as part of a comprehensive treatment plan. Our providers will help determine which supplements may be beneficial for your specific needs and how they can complement other treatments.

Always consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen, especially if you're taking medications or have existing health conditions.
