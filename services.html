Ketamine Therapy

Innovative treatment for depression, anxiety, and PTSD

About Ketamine Therapy

Ketamine therapy has emerged as a groundbreaking treatment for treatment-resistant depression, anxiety, PTSD, and chronic pain. At ReThink Mental Health, we offer professionally administered ketamine infusions in a safe, comfortable environment.

Benefits of Ketamine Therapy

  • Rapid relief from depressive symptoms, often within hours or days
  • Effective for treatment-resistant conditions
  • May reduce suicidal thoughts
  • Can break the cycle of negative thinking
  • Potential for long-lasting results with maintenance treatments

Our Ketamine Treatment Process

Each ketamine treatment session is carefully monitored by our medical professionals. The typical treatment course involves a series of infusions over several weeks, followed by maintenance treatments as needed. We create individualized treatment plans based on your specific needs and response to therapy.

Is Ketamine Therapy Right for You?

If you've struggled with treatment-resistant depression or other mental health conditions, ketamine therapy might be an option worth exploring.

Spravato Treatment

FDA-approved intranasal ketamine for treatment-resistant depression

What is Spravato?

Spravato (esketamine) is an FDA-approved nasal spray for treatment-resistant depression and major depressive disorder with suicidal thoughts. As a REMS certified center, ReThink Mental Health offers this innovative treatment in a controlled, supportive environment.

How Spravato Works

Spravato works differently than traditional antidepressants. While most antidepressants work on serotonin, norepinephrine, or dopamine, Spravato targets glutamate, the most abundant chemical messenger in the brain. This novel mechanism can provide relief for patients who haven't responded to other treatments.

The Spravato Experience

  • Treatment is administered in our office under medical supervision
  • Each session lasts approximately 2 hours
  • You'll need someone to drive you home after treatment
  • Initial treatment phase includes twice-weekly sessions for 4 weeks
  • Maintenance treatment schedule is personalized based on your response

Considering Spravato Treatment?

Our team can help determine if Spravato is appropriate for your condition and guide you through the treatment process.

Testing & Diagnostics

Comprehensive testing for personalized treatment

Advanced Diagnostic Services

At ReThink Mental Health, we believe in the power of precision medicine. Our comprehensive testing and diagnostic services help us understand the unique factors contributing to your mental health concerns, allowing for more targeted and effective treatment plans.

Our Testing Services Include

  • Genetic Testing: Pharmacogenetic testing to identify how your genes may affect medication response
  • QbTesting for ADHD: Objective assessment of attention, impulsivity, and activity
  • Laboratory Testing: Comprehensive blood work to identify nutritional deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, and other factors that may impact mental health
  • Imaging Studies: When appropriate, we may recommend brain imaging to rule out structural issues
  • Psychological Assessments: Standardized tests to evaluate mood, anxiety, trauma, and cognitive function

The Benefits of Comprehensive Testing

Our diagnostic approach can help reduce the trial-and-error process often associated with mental health treatment. By identifying biological, genetic, and psychological factors specific to you, we can develop more targeted treatment plans with better outcomes.
