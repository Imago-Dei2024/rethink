About ReThink Mental Health

Transforming mental health care through innovation and compassion

Our Story

ReThink Mental Health was founded with a vision to transform mental health care by combining traditional psychiatric approaches with innovative therapies and wellness practices. We recognized that many individuals weren't finding relief through conventional treatments alone, and we set out to create a more comprehensive model of care.

Our practice began with a focus on providing specialized care for first responders, frontline healthcare workers, and military personnel—communities that face unique mental health challenges. As we grew, we expanded our services to include cutting-edge treatments like ketamine therapy and Spravato, while maintaining our commitment to personalized, evidence-based care for all patients.

Today, ReThink Mental Health stands as a leader in integrative psychiatry in the Denver area, offering a wide range of services designed to address the full spectrum of mental health needs. We continue to evolve our practice based on the latest research and innovations in the field, always with the goal of providing the most effective care possible.

Our Mission & Vision

Team Meeting Image

Mission

To provide compassionate, innovative, and evidence-based mental health care that addresses the unique needs of each individual, empowering them to achieve lasting wellness and live fulfilling lives.

Vision

We envision a world where mental health care is accessible, effective, and free from stigma—where innovative treatments and integrative approaches are available to all who need them, and where each person's journey to wellness is supported with dignity and respect.

Our Approach

At ReThink Mental Health, we believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms. Our integrative approach combines:

  • Traditional psychiatric care and medication management
  • Innovative treatments like ketamine therapy and Spravato
  • Comprehensive diagnostic testing to identify underlying factors
  • Nutritional and wellness support
  • Specialized care for unique populations

We work collaboratively with each patient to develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses their specific needs and goals, drawing from our diverse range of services to create the most effective approach.

Our Journey

The evolution of ReThink Mental Health reflects our commitment to innovation and excellence in mental health care.

2018

The Beginning

ReThink Mental Health was founded with a focus on providing specialized mental health care for first responders and healthcare workers.

2019

Expanding Services

We expanded our services to include ketamine therapy and comprehensive diagnostic testing, broadening our approach to treatment-resistant conditions.

2020

REMS Certification

We became a REMS certified center for Spravato administration, adding another innovative treatment option for our patients.

2021

Wellness Integration

We introduced our wellness services, including nutrition counseling and micronutrient injections, embracing a more holistic approach to mental health.

2022

New Facility

We moved to our current location, a purpose-designed facility that provides a healing environment for all our services.

2023

Community Outreach

We launched our community education program, offering workshops and resources to reduce mental health stigma and increase awareness.

2024

Research Participation

We began participating in clinical research to advance the field of mental health and provide our patients with access to cutting-edge treatments.

2025

Looking Forward

We continue to grow and evolve, always seeking new ways to improve our care and better serve our community.
